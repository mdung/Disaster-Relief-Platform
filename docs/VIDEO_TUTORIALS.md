## ðŸŽ¬ Video Production Guidelines

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Audio**: 48 kHz, 16-bit stereo
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Duration**: 2-10 minutes per video
- **File Size**: < 100MB per video

### Recording Setup
- **Screen Recording**: OBS Studio or similar
- **Audio**: External microphone (Blue Yeti or similar)
- **Lighting**: Soft, even lighting
- **Background**: Clean, professional background
- **Browser**: Chrome or Firefox (latest version)

### Post-Production
- **Editing**: Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve
- **Graphics**: Consistent branding and overlays
- **Transitions**: Smooth, professional transitions
- **Audio**: Noise reduction and level normalization
- **Captions**: Closed captions for accessibility

#### Account Creation (0:30 - 1:30)
```
"To get started, you'll need to create an account. Click the 
'Sign Up' button on the main page and fill in your information.

You'll need to provide:

- Email address

```

#### Profile Setup (1:30 - 2:00)
```
"After creating your account, you'll need to set up your profile.

- Update your contact information
- Add emergency contacts
- Set notification preferences
- Configure privacy settings
- Upload a profile photo

Keeping your profile up to date ensures that responders can

```

#### Submitting Needs (2:00 - 4:00)
```
"To submit a needs request, click on 'Submit Need' in the main 
navigation. You'll see a form where you can describe your needs.

Fill in the following information:
- Need category (food, water, medical, shelter, etc.)
- Description of what you need
- Severity level (1-5, with 5 being most urgent)
- Your location (address or GPS coordinates)
- Contact information
- Any additional details

You can also upload photos or documents to help describe your 
situation. This information helps dispatchers understand your 
needs and assign appropriate resources."
```

#### Tracking Status (4:00 - 5:00)
```

on 'My Needs' to see all your submitted requests.

You'll see the current status of each need:
- New: Just submitted, awaiting review
- Under Review: Being assessed by dispatchers
- Assigned: Task created and assigned to helper
- In Progress: Helper is working on your need
- Completed: Your need has been addressed
- Closed: Final resolution and documentation

You'll receive notifications when your need status changes, 
and you can communicate with dispatchers if you have questions."
```
